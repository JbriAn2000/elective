Quick Overview

CleanMate is a platform designed to facilitate the reporting and viewing of trash in our environment. By using CleanMate, you can contribute to keeping our surroundings clean and healthy. Whether you want to report a new instance of trash or view existing reports, CleanMate provides the tools you need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to take action in maintaining a clean environment. We believe that by providing a user-friendly platform for reporting and viewing trash, we can collectively make a significant impact on the health of our planet. Join us in our mission to create a cleaner, healthier world for everyone.
