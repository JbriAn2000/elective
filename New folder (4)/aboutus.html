Our Mission

CleanMate is dedicated to fostering cleaner, healthier communities by empowering individuals to report and address environmental issues. Our mission is to create a platform that not only facilitates the reporting of trash and pollution but also educates and engages the community in sustainable practices. We are committed to supporting Sustainable Development Goal 11, aiming to make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Community Impact

CleanMate has facilitated the reporting and cleanup of numerous instances of environmental pollution. Our community members have organized cleanup events, contributing volunteer hours to make neighborhoods cleaner and safer.
