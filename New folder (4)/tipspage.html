Tips

Reporting Guidelines

When reporting, provide clear photos of the issue, including the location and any identifying features.

Include the exact address or cross-streets of the issue to help the authorities locate it quickly.

Describe the problem in detail, including the type of trash, size, and any potential hazards.

If possible, estimate the size or amount of trash to help authorities prioritize the cleanup.

Community Cleanup Tips

Organize community cleanup events with neighbors to tackle larger areas and promote teamwork.

Use social media and local groups to spread awareness about cleanup events and encourage participation.

Provide gloves, trash bags, and other necessary supplies to participants for a safe and effective cleanup.

Properly dispose of collected trash according to local regulations to ensure environmental safety.
