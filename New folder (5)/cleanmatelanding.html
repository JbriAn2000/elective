How CleanMate Works

Our platform simplifies the process of reporting and resolving community cleanliness issues.

Report a Location

Easily pinpoint and report dirty areas on a map with detailed descriptions and photos.

Community Collaboration

Connect with neighbors and local organizations to coordinate cleanup efforts and share updates.

Resolution Tracking

Track the progress of reported issues from submission to resolution, ensuring accountability and transparency.
