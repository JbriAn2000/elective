Reporting Issues Effectively

Report Through the App

Use the app's 'Report' feature to submit details about the issue.

Provide Visual Evidence

Include photos or videos to show the problem clearly.

Specify the Location

Describe the location precisely for quick identification.

Add Detailed Descriptions

Add any extra details that might help understand the issue.

Proper Waste Disposal

Dispose of trash properly in designated bins.
